About MusicQEARY

MusicQEARY lets you see a visual representation of the musical attributes of your Spotify Playlists and Songs. We use Spotify's API to get a variety of data about the music you listen to and create playlist averages and user averages for your audio data.

We look at the following data:

Danceability
Tempo, rhytmic stability, strength of beat, and regularity.
Energy
Speed, loudness, and noisiness.
Instrumentalness
Lack of vocals in a track ("Ooh" and "aah" do not count as vocals for this feature)
Liveness
The presence of an audience in the recording.
Speechiness
Presence of spoken words in the track.
Valence
Positiveness of the track (higher valence tracks sound more happy).
Acousticness
The presence of acoustic instruments in the track.
